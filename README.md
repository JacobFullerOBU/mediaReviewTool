# TrueRated 🎬 🎮 📚 🎵 📺

Tired of critic scores that don't match what anyone actually thinks? TrueRated is built around real opinions from real people. This site allows covering movies, TV, music, games, and books all in one place.

🔗 **[truerated.co](https://truerated.co/)**

---

## What it does

You can browse reviews, leave your own take, and filter down to exactly what you're in the mood for. Not sure what to watch tonight? Hit **Surprise Me** and let the site decide for you.

Right now movies support anonymous reviews so you can jump straight in without making an account. More categories and full auth are on the way.

---

## Features

- **Browse & filter** — jump between Movies, TV, Music, Games, and Books instantly
- **Sort your way** — Highest Rated, Newest, Oldest, Alphabetical — whatever makes sense to you
- **Surprise Me** — one click, one random pick, no more decision paralysis
- **Works everywhere** — designed to feel good on your phone, tablet, or desktop
