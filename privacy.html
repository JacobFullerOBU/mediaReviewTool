Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

1. Overview

True Rated ("we," "us," or "our") operates the True Rated website. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. By using True Rated, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information: If you create an account, we collect your email address and display name.
  • User-generated content: Reviews, ratings, and comments you submit are stored and may be displayed publicly.
  • Usage data: We may collect information about how you interact with the site (pages visited, features used) through standard server logs and analytics tools.
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, and IP address collected automatically when you visit the site.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the True Rated service.
  • To display your reviews and ratings to other users.
  • To send service-related communications (account verification, updates).
  • To improve our site's features and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser local storage and session cookies to:

  • Keep you signed in between sessions.
  • Cache media scores and review data to speed up page loads.
  • Remember your filter and sort preferences.

You can clear local storage at any time through your browser settings, though this will sign you out and reset any cached preferences.

5. Third-Party Services

True Rated integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google): We use Firebase Authentication and Realtime Database to manage accounts and store reviews. Google's privacy policy applies to data processed through Firebase.
  • The Movie Database (TMDB): We fetch movie and TV metadata and scores from TMDB's public API.
  • OMDb API: We use OMDb to retrieve Rotten Tomatoes scores and additional media information.
  • Tailwind CSS CDN / Lucide Icons CDN: These CDNs may collect standard request logs when serving assets to your browser.

6. Affiliate Disclosure

True Rated participates in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com. When you click an Amazon link on this site and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. We only link to products relevant to the media being reviewed.

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may disclose information only in the following circumstances:

  • To comply with a legal obligation or court order.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of True Rated, our users, or the public.
  • With service providers (e.g., Firebase/Google) that help us operate the site, under confidentiality agreements.

8. Data Retention

We retain your account data and reviews for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal information from our active systems, though some data may remain in backup copies for a limited period as required by law.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt out of any future marketing communications.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

10. Children's Privacy

True Rated is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
